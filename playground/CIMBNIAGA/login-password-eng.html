or virtual keyboard (?) About Virtual Keyboard

A virtual keyboard is a full-size image of a QWERTY keyboard projected onto your computer monitor. To use it, simply and click your mouse to the image od a key.

For your security, it is suggested to use virtual keyboard to enter your password as it reduces the risk of keystroke logging. It is more difficult for malware to monitor the display and mouse to obtain the data entered via the virtual keyboard, than it is to monitor real keystrokes.
